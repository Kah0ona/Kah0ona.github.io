About Marten Sytema

I am a Software Engineer & Enterpreneur from The Hague, The Netherlands.

Enterpreneurship

I run a software company called Sytematic Software. I graduated on my own business idea at Twente University, Enschede in 2010, and started my company right after graduation.

My trusty code-generator, RADOS

My master thesis was about a model-driven code generator, RADOS. RADOS converts a class diagram into a fully functioning web-application written in Google Web Toolkit, backed by a MySQL database. There is a hook registration mechanism for customizing the generated code. RADOS can also generate a REST-API for the apps. Pretty neat.

I graduated with 9 out of 10 score, and I am quite proud of the generator. It's still actively used today.

What I've built with RADOS

  • A very complex custom student/course management system for Technicom, an educational institute.
  • Social Dipping, a web-app for ice cream parlours providing a virtual dipping cabinet
  • Catering Software, a web-app backend for a webshop with domain-specific functionality for the catering business, like shopping lists, kitchen lists, etc.
  • Partnering with Lokaal Gevonden, a SEO company, we sell webshops. The backend of the webshop was built with RADOS. It integrates into Wordpress CMS, where a plugin pulls the products.
